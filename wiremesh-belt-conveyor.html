Wiremesh Belt Conveyor

Wiremesh Belt Conveyors are robust, open-surface conveying systems designed for extreme operating conditions, including high temperatures, wet environments, and sharp-edged materials. Constructed with stainless steel or galvanized wire mesh belts, these conveyors offer excellent airflow, drainage, and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for industries with demanding processes.

This design allows the system to handle:

  • Hot workpieces
  • Sharp-edged materials
  • Heavy and wide loads, without requiring high belt tension

The wire mesh design allows stable transport of small to large parts while enabling cooling, drying, washing, or heating operations directly on the conveyor belt.

Types Available
  • Straight Wiremesh Conveyors
  • Incline/Decline Conveyors
  • Multi-Tier or Tunnel Conveyors
  • Customized Layouts with Side Guards or Supports

Application
