Steady

Steady

Series 1300 submersible pumps are engineered for dependable and efficient performance in sewage and dewatering applications. Proven in demanding conditions worldwide, these pumps feature a robust, non-clogging impeller and advanced hydraulic design that help maintain high efficiency while minimizing blockages during wastewater handling. With horsepower options ranging from 1.2 to 78 HP (0.89 to 58.16 kW) and operating speeds from 1150 to 3500 RPM, the Steady 1300 Series offers maximum flow rates between 130 and 8200 GPM (29.5 to 18,530.4 L/min), and heads from 33 to 250 feet (10.06 to 76.2 m). Discharge outlets are available in sizes from 2” to 12” (50.8 to 304.8 mm), making this series a versatile and high-performing solution for a wide range of wastewater applications.
