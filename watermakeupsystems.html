Water Make-Up Systems & Water Treatment

Automatic make-up systems ensure consistent water levels, while water treatment technologies such as softening and demineralisation protect against scaling, corrosion, and premature wear. Support optimal performance and reliability in heating, cooling and process water systems. Maintain system stability, efficiency and longevity.

Water Make-Up Systems & Water Treatment

Fillset

Fillset water make-up valves ensure system separation in accordance with DIN EN 1717, allowing a permanent, safe connection between heating or cooling systems and potable water networks. They can be employed for direct manual make-up or expanded into fully automatic solutions using Fillset Safecontrol motorised ball valves paired with Reflex Control controllers. Reflex offers several Fillset variants and extensions to suit diverse requirements: the space-saving Fillset Compact without a water meter, the Fillset Combi includes a pulse generator and contact water meter and is compatible with Reflex Control units.

Fillcontrol

Fill Control is an automatic water make-up unit designed for use with static pressure expansion vessels, featuring a dual-pump configuration for enhanced reliability. It includes a microprocessor-based control panel with a 2-line LCD display and pressure transducer for precise monitoring and control. The system is equipped with an 18-litre break tank, along with a WRAS-approved ball valve and a Type AB air gap to ensure safe and compliant operation.

Fillcontrol Auto

Fillcontrol Auto series offers fully automatic water make-up systems with integrated pumps for reliable and efficient operation. The Fillcontrol Auto Compact model features an integrated intermediate tank to ensure system separation in compliance with DIN 1988 and DIN EN 1717. These systems are equipped with the Control Basic interface for simple and intuitive operation and include an RS 485 interface that supports connection to bus and extension modules. Capacity monitoring of an associated Fillsoft water treatment system is possible enhancing overall system management and safety.

Fillsoft

Fillsoft cartridges are available in two versions green and grey, designed to fit their corresponding Fillsoft housings. The green cartridge functions as a water softener, effectively reducing hardness by removing calcium and magnesium ions to protect plumbing and appliances. The grey cartridge serves as a desalination unit, targeting the removal of salts and impurities to improve water quality. Together with the Fillsoft housing, these cartridges provide tailored water treatment solutions suitable for a range of residential and commercial applications, ensuring optimal system performance and longevity.
