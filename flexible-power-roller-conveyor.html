Flexible Power Roller Conveyor

The Flexible Power Roller Conveyor from Flextrack is a dynamic, motorized conveying solution designed for maximum flexibility, mobility, and efficiency. Built with extendable and bendable frame sections, this conveyor adapts easily to changing floor layouts and operational needs, making it ideal for loading/unloading, sorting, and temporary transfer operations.

Each roller is independently powered, ensuring smooth, continuous flow of goods even along curves and inclines. The conveyor’s adjustable length and shape allow it to be expanded, contracted, or curved as needed, while durable casters provide easy maneuverability.

Application
  • Perfect for industries requiring fast and flexible material handling:
  • E-commerce Fulfillment Centers
  • Logistics & Warehousing
  • Courier & Parcel Services
  • Packaging Lines
  • Food and Beverage Distribution
  • Loading/Unloading from Trucks or Containers
