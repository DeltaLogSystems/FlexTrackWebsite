About Us

Flextrack Belting is a leading manufacturer of innovative conveyor systems and belting solutions with over 8 years of expertise. We specialize in delivering high-quality, customized conveyor belts, rollers, and accessories for a wide range of industries, including manufacturing, logistics, and packaging. Our commitment to precision engineering and sustainability ensures efficient, reliable, and durable solutions tailored to meet our clients' unique needs.

Vision

To be the global leader in conveyor and belting solutions, driving innovation and sustainability in material handling systems across industries.

Mission

To design and deliver high-performance conveyor systems and belting solutions that optimize operational efficiency, enhance productivity, and ensure customer satisfaction across diverse industrial applications.

Our History/Voyage:

Founded in 2017, Flextrack Belting has established itself as a trusted name in conveyor manufacturing. From our beginnings as a small-scale belt producer, we have grown into a global provider of comprehensive conveyor solutions, including belt conveyors, roller conveyors, screw conveyors, and specialized packaging line systems. Our journey is marked by a dedication to innovation, quality, and sustainable practices that empower our clients' operations.

We offer a comprehensive portfolio of conveyor systems and belting solutions, designed to meet the diverse needs of modern industries.

Belt Conveyors

Flextrack Belting specializes in a wide range of belt conveyors, including PU/PVC, modular plastic, wire mesh, and loading/unloading conveyors. Our belt conveyors are engineered for durability, efficiency, and seamless integration into manufacturing, logistics, and distribution systems, ensuring optimal material handling performance.

Roller and Screw Conveyors

Our roller conveyors, including power, flexible, skate wheel, and gravity systems, are designed for versatility and reliability in material transport. Additionally, our horizontal and incline screw conveyors offer robust solutions for bulk material handling, delivering precision and efficiency across industries such as agriculture, food processing, and construction.

Belts and Accessories

Flextrack Belting provides a diverse range of belts, including modular, PU/PVC, rubber, and wire mesh options, tailored to specific operational needs. Our conveyor and belt accessories enhance system performance, offering customized solutions for maintenance, safety, and efficiency in applications ranging from packaging lines to heavy-duty industrial processes.
