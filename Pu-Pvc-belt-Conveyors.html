PU/PVC Belt Conveyor

Our PU and PVC Belt Conveyors are designed for efficient and safe material transfer across a wide range of industries. These belts are manufactured in various colors such as white (food grade), green, and black, and are available in 1-ply, 2-ply, and 3-ply configurations with thickness ranging from 2mm to 6mm.

PU (Polyurethane) belts are 100% non-toxic and FDA approved, making them ideal for direct food contact. They offer superior mechanical strength and chemical resistance compared to conventional belts.

PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) belts are widely used for lightweight to moderate load handling, offering excellent flexibility and durability.
