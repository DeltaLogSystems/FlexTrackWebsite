Bell & Gossett

Bell & Gossett – e80SC – Split Coupled Vertical Inline Pump

Series e-80SC and e-80SCX Smart Pumps integrate decades of expertise and know-how in pumping solutions to bring the right combination of motors, variable speed drives and hydraulic pumps in one comprehensive, highly efficient package. Series e-80SC and e-80SCX Smart Pumps reduce electricity consumption, improve overall system performance, and lower life cycle costs. Designed for horizontal and vertical in-line mounting, it’s ideal for hydronic heating and cooling systems, light industrial processes and general service.

Bell & Gossett – 1510 – End Suction Back Pullout Design Pump

Series 1510 Base-Mounted End-Suction Pumps available in sizes from 1-1/4” to 8” with motor options ranging from 1/2 HP to 150 HP at 1750 RPM, and 2 HP to 150 HP at 3500 RPM, these pumps support flow rates up to 4000 GPM and heads up to 500 feet. Offered in bronze-fitted or all-iron material configurations, the Series 1510 features an integrally cast volute foot and center drop-out spacer coupling for true back pullout capability—minimizing downtime and maintenance costs. Additional standard features include an ANSI/OSHA-compliant coupling guard, internally flushed mechanical seal, and a robust welded steel baseplate.

Bell Gossett Horizontal Split Case Pumps

Series HSC-S is a long-coupled, base-mounted, single-stage, double-suction pump with a horizontally split case design, cast iron body, and bronze-fitted construction, specifically engineered for reliable and quiet operation. Available in sizes from 2” to 10” with motor capacities up to 300 HP, it delivers flows up to 6,500 GPM and heads to 600 feet. Designed for standard operation at 225°F (107°C) and 175 psig (12 BAR), it also supports optional high-performance operation up to 250°F (121°C) and pressures of 280 or 400 psig (19 or 27 BAR), without de-rating at elevated temperatures. The pump features externally flushed mechanical seals and a durable closed-end steel baseplate with full seam welds and an open top for simplified grouting. Horizontal (Model 150) and vertical (Model 300) configurations are available to suit diverse installation needs. For ease of maintenance, all internal components, including bearings and mechanical seals, can be accessed without disturbing piping or motor connections. An ANSI/OSHA-compliant coupling guard provides added safety for the flexible coupler.
