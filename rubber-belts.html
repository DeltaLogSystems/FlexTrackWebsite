Rubber Belt

Flextrack Belting is a leading manufacturer and supplier of premium rubber belts designed for high-efficiency power transmission and industrial motion control. Our rubber belts are engineered to perform reliably in high-load, high-speed, and high-temperature conditions — ideal for industries such as automotive, mining, agriculture, textiles, cement, and more.

Manufactured from high-quality synthetic rubber and reinforced with cords or fabric layers, Flextrack’s rubber belts ensure excellent grip, flexibility, and vibration resistance.

These belts are ideal for applications requiring rotation, movement, or synchronization — excluding conveyors — such as drive systems, machinery linkage, and pulley-based systems.
