Hot Water Storage Tanks & Heat Exchangers

Hot water storage tanks and heat exchangers are essential components in heating and cooling systems. Hot water storage tanks store and maintain heated water for use in domestic, commercial or industrial applications, ensuring a steady supply when needed. Heat exchangers transfer heat efficiently between two fluids without mixing them, enabling effective heating or cooling while maximizing energy use. 

Hot Water Storage Tanks & Heat Exchangers

Storatherm Aqua

Storatherm Aqua tanks are designed for heating and storing potable water, serving a wide range of applications in residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. With various models and a comprehensive selection of accessories, they offer versatile solutions for modern building services. For long-lasting corrosion protection, tanks are coated with hygienic enamel in accordance with DIN 4753 standards.

Storatherm Heat Buffer Tank

Storatherm heat buffer tanks function similar to stratification tanks, acting as thermal batteries that decouple heat generation from heat consumption—both hydraulically and over time. This makes them ideal for integrating heat sources like waste heat, solar systems, and CHP units, whose output doesn't always align with demand. Versatile in application, they primarily serve as central storage units for heating systems, ensuring stable and efficient energy supply.

Sinus Buffer Tanks

Sinus buffer tanks are designed in accordance with DGRL and approved per Sinus works standards or AD 2000 regulations. Available in sizes up to DN 2,400 and 25,000 litres, they are suitable for both hot and cold water systems. Connections are configured to meet individual specifications, with optional features including pipe bends, nozzle pipes or mixing devices.

Longtherm Heat Exchangers

Longtherm brazed and gasketed heat exchanger models offer efficient and reliable thermal transfer solutions for a wide range of applications. The brazed models feature a special soldering process that ensures permanent plate bonding, allowing them to withstand high temperatures up to 230 °C and operating pressures up to 30 bar. Gasketed models are ideal for systems with very low temperature differentials and high output requirements exceeding 1 MW. Their modular, dismantlable design allows for easy cleaning and maintenance, with the flexibility to adjust the number of plates as needed.
