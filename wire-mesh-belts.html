Wire Mesh Belt

Flextrack Belting specializes in the design and supply of premium Wire Mesh Belts for industrial process applications. Crafted using high-grade stainless steel or mild steel, our wire mesh belts are ideal for use in high-temperature, corrosive, and heavy-load environments — without the need for a complete conveyor system.

These belts are used in industries such as heat treatment, metalworking, food processing, baking, drying, washing, and glass manufacturing. They are engineered for smooth operation, air and liquid flow, and exceptional durability, even under extreme thermal and mechanical stress.

Available in multiple configurations like balanced weave, compound weave, chain-driven, and flat wire, our wire mesh belts are customizable to suit your exact process needs.

Application
  • Heat treatment & furnace operations
  • Food baking, freezing, and drying lines
  • Pharmaceutical sterilization
  • Glass and ceramics handling
  • Metalworking and powder coating
  • Washing and degreasing systems
  • Industrial filtration and separation
