For potable water applications

REFIX is specially curated for potable water and hydro-pneumatic well applications, providing reliable and efficient water management solutions. Designed to meet stringent safety and quality standards, systems ensure the consistent delivery of clean, safe water by preventing contamination and maintaining optimal pressure levels. Ideal for both residential and commercial uses.

For potable water applications

Refix C-DE

Refix C-DE vessel features threaded stainless steel connections and corrosion-protected components in contact with water. It includes a suspension bracket for convenient wall mounting. The non-replaceable butyl bladder complies with DIN EN 13831/3 and supports a maximum operating temperature of 70°C. The vessel meets or exceeds the requirements of Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/EU and is finished with a durable epoxy resin coating. Additionally, it comes factory pre-pressurised with a nitrogen gas chamber for optimal performance.

Refix DC

Refix DC is vessel features threaded connections and corrosion-resistant components in contact with water. It includes a non-replaceable diaphragm compliant with DIN EN 13831/3, supporting a maximum operating temperature of 70°C. The vessel meets or exceeds the requirements of Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/EU and is finished with a durable epoxy resin coating. Additional features include a factory pre-pressurised nitrogen gas chamber and lifting lugs for ease of handling.

Refix DE

Refix DE vessel offers threaded connections up to 1,000 litres and flanged connections above 1,000 litres, with 3,000 to 10,000-litre models featuring an upper flange. It includes a bladder per DIN EN 13831/3, max operating temperature 70°C. From 60 litres, bladders are replaceable; from 50 litres, vessels have feet. A factory-mounted pressure gauge is provided from Ø 1,000 mm. Components in contact with water are corrosion-protected. The vessel meets or exceeds Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/EU, has a long-lasting epoxy coating, a factory pre-pressurised nitrogen gas chamber and lifting lugs

Refix HW

Suitable for potable water and hydro-pneumatic well applications, this vessel features a bladder compliant with DIN EN 13831/3 from 50 litres, supporting a maximum operating temperature of 70°C. Components in contact with water are corrosion-protected. The vessel meets or exceeds Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/EU requirements, is coated with durable epoxy resin and includes a factory pre-pressurised nitrogen gas chamber.

Refix DE1

Refix DE1 is designed to use with devices that experience sudden pressure build-up from rapid shut-off, such as washing machines and dishwashers, this vessel complies with or exceeds EC Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/EU standards. It has a volume of 1 litre, features a durable epoxy resin coating and includes a factory pre-pressurised nitrogen gas chamber at 4 bar. The vessel is rated for a maximum pressure of 10 bar and an operating temperature up to 70°C.

Refix Water Shock Arrestor

Refix Water Shock Arrestor application involves sudden pressure spikes caused by rapid shut-off devices such as washing machines and dishwashers, this vessel meets or exceeds the requirements of the EC Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/EU. With a capacity of 1 litre, it features a durable epoxy resin coating and a factory pre-pressurised nitrogen gas chamber at 4 bar. The vessel is rated for a maximum pressure of 10 bar and an operating temperature up to 70°C.

Refix DD

Reflex DD is specifically designed for potable water applications and complies with German DIN/DVGW 4807/5 standards. It features a non-replaceable butyl bladder in accordance with DIN EN 13831/3 and supports a maximum operating temperature of 70°C. The vessel meets or exceeds Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/EU requirements and includes an inner anti-corrosion coating as per German KTW-A foodstuff regulations. Reflex DD can be installed with a flow-through valve (Flowjet) and is connectable to a T-piece Rp 3/4", which is included in the delivery. It is coated with a long-lasting epoxy resin and factory pre-pressurised with a nitrogen gas chamber.

Refix DT

Reflex DT is designed for potable water applications in accordance with German DIN/DVGW 4807/5 and features a replaceable butyl bladder as per DIN EN 13831/3, with a maximum operating temperature of 70°C. It complies with the Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/EU and includes an inner anti-corrosion coating in line with German KTW-A foodstuff regulations. Vessels with Rp 1/4" connection (60–500 litres) come factory-equipped with a Flowjet valve, which combines flow-through, shut-off, and discharge functions. A factory-mounted pressure gauge, durable epoxy resin coating, nitrogen pre-pressurised gas chamber, and lifting lugs ensure ease of handling, reliable performance, and long service life.
