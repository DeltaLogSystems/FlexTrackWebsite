Flygt mixer

Compact Single Speed Mixer

Compact Single Speed series is designed to use in constrained tanks with high-density, high-temperature, viscous or contaminated media, the solution delivers reliable performance even with low running hours. With the widest thrust range on the market (100–6800 N, Ø0.21–0.78 m) it is ideal for demanding environments such as biogas plants, drilling mud systems, and water or wastewater treatment. Built to withstand corrosive and abrasive fluids, it features durable materials like stainless steel, duplex stainless steel, or Hard-Iron blades, and is fully suitable for operation in explosion-prone zones.

Geared Single Speed Mixer

Geared Single Speed Series is Ideal for conventional treatment systems, oxidation ditches, SBRs, wastewater reservoirs, and oxygenation in lakes and harbors, solution excels in space-constrained environments where efficiency is critical. Serving both municipal and industrial markets, it offers proven reliability and outstanding energy efficiency—up to 1260 N/kW—delivering significant cost savings. Designed for non-stop performance, it features non-clogging hydraulics, long-life submersible motors, and a heavy-duty gearbox. Available in sizes ranging from 400–4500 N with a diameter of 1.2–2.5 m.

N-Technology Compact Mixer

N- Technology Compact Series is designed for mixing and flushing in retention basins, as well as applications involving cooling water, sludge, raw water, clean water, surface water, and wastewater across industrial projects, Flygt hydro-ejectors offer robust and reliable performance. With superior non-clog capabilities, self-cleaning impellers, and multi-vane designs, they ensure sustained high efficiency even in challenging wastewater conditions. Easy to install and equipped with high power density motors, these systems deliver a maximum thrust of up to 5250 N, making them ideal for demanding fluid movement tasks.

Compact Adaptive Mixers

Compact Adaptive Mixer series is engineered for treatment and wastewater applications, delivering energy savings, reduced operational costs, and exceptional flexibility. With a thrust capacity of up to 2470 N and a diameter range of 0.37–0.77 m, it ensures high efficiency and reliability through purpose-designed sealing, hydraulics, gearboxes, and motors. Explosion-proof variants are available, along with complete systems including installation equipment. Backed by Flygt’s mixing and CFD expertise, this series meets ISO 21630 compliance and testing standards, features the high-efficiency Dirigo™ system with an IE4 motor, and comes with a 15-year spare parts guarantee.

Geared Adaptive Mixer

Geared Adaptive Mixer series is designed for treatment and wastewater applications, offering significant energy savings, reduced operational costs, and unmatched flexibility. It delivers unparalleled reliability through purpose-designed sealing, hydraulics, gearboxes, and motors, with explosion-proof variants available. The series comes as complete systems including installation equipment and benefits from Flygt’s mixing and CFD expertise. Compliant with ISO 21630 standards and backed by a 15-year spare parts guarantee, it features the advanced Dirigo™ system with an IE4 motor. The size range covers thrusts up to 5180 N and diameters from 1.4 to 2.5 meters.

N-Technology Geared Mixer

N-Technology Geared Series is ideal for sludge mixing and tanks with very low liquid levels, dry-installed jet mixers are designed for on-the-ground installation in treatment and biogas applications. Featuring Flygt N-technology, they deliver sustained high efficiency with non-clogging performance. These systems ensure no process interruptions, reduced energy and maintenance costs, and low installation expenses due to their dry-mounted submersible design. With easy access for servicing, a safe working environment, and features like a patented telescopic inlet and service cart, they offer a maximum thrust of up to 5250 N.
